Unreal Engine

The goal here was to design a level but I ended up playing around with the characters

I wanted less boring characters instead of the default Unreal mannequin. That's when I discovered that the character rig could support "attachments". Attachments are normally used to stick clothes or pieces of armor onto a model. I used is to modify the default character

I made a complete cooperative horror map. But this time I modded Unreal Tournament

The goal of this exercice was to make a cooperative level with puzzles to solve

I recently watched the SAW movies so I was inspired
First of all for the atmosphere : majority of darkness, red fog, the same texture everywhere to give an uneasy feeling and of course a creepy ambient sound(slown down wind using SoundForge)
Then I needed the players to be scared of something real. At first I thought of a countdown but I wanted something more original. Instead of the time running out it would be their lives : each time the players would go through shadows, they would lose health points

What I particulary enjoyed is the use of the Impact Hammer. Normally a melee weapon. If you hit the ground below you with the weapon, It propulses you in the air at a cost of life points. I used this mechanic in one of the puzzles to get in higher ground
