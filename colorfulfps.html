First Person Shooter Demo

For this demo I focused on first person gun animations

First of all, the guns are using raycasts for their shooting

The gun recoil you see are not key-framed animations, these are procedural animations

A each shot, the gun will move back at a random value with a threshold. Same thing with rotation.

I then re-used the particle from another scene for the hit effect on the target we shot

With the instancing of the blood particle, I apply a force on impact

To make it prettier, I added a trail particle coming out of the barrel at high speed. These trails can collide and bounce on collisions

At the end I added some post-processing effects such as chromatic abberation(my favorite), vignette, and a color filter
